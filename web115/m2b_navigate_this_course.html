M2B—Navigate This Course

Items From Main Course Menu

Content
Clicking this tab brings one to an Overview of the course. It describes what the student will be learning, describes the expected takeaways from the course, and gives an indication of how much student effort will be required to successfully complete the course. This page is only useful at the beginning of the course and won't be used weekly.
Discussions
This page, unlike the Content page, will get heavy usage. All assignments save weekly quizzes and blog posts are likely to be submitted on this page. Of particular importance is the Class Discussion link, where questions about assignments or questions for the instructor in general are posted. As stated on the page, this is the classroom.
Class Progress
This page gives a detailed breakdown of the student's current progress in the course. It is segmented into the relevant menu items from the nav banner. Within each section is a drop-down menu that gives further details about progress in that menu item. This page will be helpful in figuring out whether the student has done the proper peer review as the Discussions section of this page lists all of the student's authored posts.

Three Other Items

Course Tools
This link pulls up a drop-down list that contains some of the other links on the nav banner in addition to a couple of new ones. Most of the links here aren't currently useful or are redundant, but one important one here is the link to the Blog. Since we're required to post to the Blog weekly, and this is the only place I've found a link to it, Course Tools will be used at least weekly.
Update Alerts
A button consisting of an image of a bell is located above the nav banner. This button has an orange circle on it when certain events occur, such as a grade being updated or a response to one of the student's posts is submitted. This button will make it easier to see when someone has received a response as compared to poking through the Discussions page to find these things out.
Subscription Alerts
This button is just to the left of the Update Alerts button and has similar functionality. The difference is that this button notifies the student when a thread they've subscribed to has been updated. (On each post, the student can click a check box to Subscribe to that particular thread.)
