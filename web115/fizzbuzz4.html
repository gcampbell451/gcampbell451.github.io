Welcome to Campbell Ink!

Hello! Below is a form you can use to test the divisibility of any three numbers you choose. Enter your name in the first three fields, how high you would like to count in the fourth, and three numbers/words in the subsequent fields. Clicking "Submit" at the bottom of the page will test each number as the count begins from 1 to the number you choose. If the number(s) is(are) divisible by the count index, the associated word(s) you chose will display. For example, if you choose 2 as your First Number and blue as your First Word, blue will print after every even number in your count. If the count index is not divisible by any of the three numbers you enter, the Default Word will be printed.
