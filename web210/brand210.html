Campbell Ink — Brand Page

The concept of my brand is a play on the pronunciation of the abbreviation for the word "incorporated." When I first began thinking about a brand one of my first ideas was "Campbell, Inc." Given that artistic elements are a main component of web design, I soon came across the idea of "Campbell Ink."
