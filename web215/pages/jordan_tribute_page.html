Michael Jeffrey Jordan

February 17, 1963—present

Listing all of Jordan's achievements is beyond the scope of this page. Some of his major accomplishments include:
  • 1982 National Champion, UNC—hit game-winning shot
  • Two-time NCAA First Team All-American (1983, 1984)
  • Naismith and Wooden College Player of the Year (1984)
  • NBA Rookie of the Year (1985)
  • Six-time NBA Champion and Finals MVP(1991-93, 1996-98)
  • Five-time NBA MVP (1988, 1991-92, 1996, 1998)
  • NBA Defensive Player of the Year (1988)
  • Ten-time NBA Scoring Leader (1987-1993m 1996-98)
  • 14-time NBA All-Star (1985-1993, 1996-98, 2002-03
  • Nine-time NBA All-Defensive First Team (1988-1993, 1996-98)
  • Two-time NBA Slam Dunk Champion (1987-88)
In 2006, Jordan bought a minority stake in the Charlotte Bobcats. Since then, he became the majority owner and changed the team's nickname back to the Hornets.

For more information, see his Wikipedia page.
